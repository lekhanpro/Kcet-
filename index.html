Understanding the Option Entry Process

Option entry is a crucial step where you get to choose the colleges and courses you are interested in, in the order of your preference. The seat allotment is done based on your rank and the options you have entered.

How to Fill Choices

  • Login to the KEA portal with your CET number.
  • You can enter any number of options.
  • The options should be in the order of your preference.
  • You can modify your choices before the last date.

Tips for Effective Option Entry

  • Research colleges and their cut-off ranks from previous years.
  • Prioritize courses based on your interest and career goals.
  • Don't hesitate to add a large number of options to be safe.
  • Take a printout of your filled options for future reference.

The Four Choices After Seat Allotment

After the first round of seat allotment, you will be given four choices. It's important to understand each choice before making a decision.

Choice 1

Satisfied with the allotted seat. You will not be considered for further rounds.

Choice 2

Satisfied with the allotted seat but wish to participate in the next round.

Choice 3

Not satisfied with the allotted seat and wish to participate in the next round.

Choice 4

Not satisfied with the allotted seat and wish to quit the counseling process.
